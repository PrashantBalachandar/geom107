About me:

My name is Textuary Gecko. I live in Wakanda on planet Centauri. I have done my masters in robotics engineering and specialize in creating intergalactic weapons for Nova Corps. Nova Corps is an organisation who protects Wakandan people and attacks the deadly outer space aliens. I'm a scientist who now spends most of my time in creating blueprints and models of robots which a soldier can drive as well as fight with it. By giving a positronic brain to the car, it can transform into a robot who can assist the soldiers to fight aliens in outer space as well as during the invasion to different planets.
